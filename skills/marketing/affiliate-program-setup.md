# Skill: Affiliate Program Setup

## What It Is For

Building the infrastructure for a B2B affiliate program. Tracking links, commission structure, payout process, and the system for recruiting and managing affiliates.

## When to Use It

When you have a product with ACV over $5K and can identify people with audiences of your buyers.

## The Infrastructure

**Tracking:** Use Rewardful, PartnerStack, or FirstPromoter. Each gives affiliates a unique tracking link and automatically tracks conversions and commissions.

**Commission structure:** 10-20% of first year contract value, paid on close (not on lead). Cap commissions if deals get very large to protect margins.

**Payout:** Monthly, via bank transfer or PayPal. Never pay before the customer pays you.

**Affiliate portal:** Every affiliate gets a dashboard where they can see their clicks, conversions, and earnings. Transparency builds trust.

## The Setup Prompt

```
I want to set up an affiliate program for [product]. 

Product ACV: [average contract value]
Target affiliate profile: [describe who you want as affiliates]
Commission I can afford: [percentage]

Help me:
1. Choose and set up the right affiliate tracking platform
2. Define the commission structure and payout terms
3. Create the affiliate application page
4. Write the affiliate welcome email and onboarding sequence
5. Build the tracking links and test them end to end
6. Create the affiliate dashboard and confirm they can see their data

Walk me through each step.
```

## The Vision Sell

The affiliates who drive real revenue are not motivated by commission. They are motivated because they believe in what you are building. Before pitching commission, pitch vision:

"We are building [vision]. I think you and your audience are perfectly positioned to grow with us. I want to give you [commission]% of every deal you bring in — but more importantly I want you to be part of what we are building."

## What Done Looks Like

- Affiliate tracking platform live and tested
- At least 10 affiliates signed up and active
- Each affiliate has working tracking link
- First commissions paid within 60 days
- Monthly affiliate newsletter in place
