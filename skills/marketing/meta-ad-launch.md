# Skill: Meta Ad Launch

## What It Is For

Launching paid ads on Facebook and Instagram via Meta Ads Manager. $35K/month at 16x lifetime ROAS. Meta's targeting and creative testing infrastructure makes it one of the most efficient direct-response channels at scale.

## When to Use It

When you have a product that converts on a landing page, a defined target customer with Meta-trackable demographics, and at least $1,500/month to spend on learning.

## Campaign Structure

**Awareness (top of funnel):** Broad targeting with interest layering. Video or image creative. Optimized for reach or link clicks.

**Consideration (middle of funnel):** Lookalike audiences. Optimized for landing page views or lead gen.

**Conversion (bottom of funnel):** Retargeting site visitors, video viewers, lead form openers. Optimized for conversions.

## The Launch Prompt

```
I want to launch Meta ads for [product]. Help me:

1. Design a 3-tier campaign structure (awareness, consideration, conversion)
2. Define target audiences for each tier based on: [describe your target customer]
3. Write creative briefs for 3 ad variations per tier (what visual, what copy, what CTA)
4. Set up the Meta Pixel correctly on my landing page
5. Configure conversion events (signup, purchase, lead)
6. Set the budget for the first 30 days
7. Define the testing and optimization schedule

Walk me through each step.
```

## Creative Testing System

Run 3-5 creative variations per ad set. Every 7 days:
- Turn off anything with CPA 50%+ above target
- Increase budget 20% on anything with CPA 20%+ below target
- Introduce 2 new creative variations to keep testing

## Lifetime ROAS vs. Short-Term ROAS

Meta optimizes for long-term value if you feed it the right data. Connect your CRM or billing system to Meta via Conversions API to track revenue over time — not just the initial purchase. This is how 16x lifetime ROAS is achieved: optimizing for customer lifetime value, not just day-one conversion.

## What Done Looks Like

- Pixel installed and firing on all key pages
- Conversion events confirmed in Events Manager
- 3-tier campaign structure live
- 3+ creative variations per tier in test
- First optimization review scheduled for day 7
