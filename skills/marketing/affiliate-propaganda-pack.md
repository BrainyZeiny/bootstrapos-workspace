# Skill: Affiliate Propaganda Pack

## What It Is For

Creating the materials affiliates use to promote your product. The better the materials, the more affiliates post. Most programs fail because they give affiliates nothing to work with.

## What Goes in the Pack

**1. The one-pager**
One page describing your product, who it is for, what problem it solves, and what results customers get. Written in the affiliate's voice — not corporate copy.

**2. Testimonials and case studies**
3-5 customer success stories with specific numbers. "We hired a developer in 4 days and saved $80K vs. our US hire" is infinitely more useful than "great service."

**3. Social media templates**
5-10 pre-written LinkedIn posts (or platform-relevant posts) that affiliates can customize and share. Remove all friction from posting.

**4. Email templates**
2-3 email templates for affiliates who have mailing lists.

**5. Tracking link setup instructions**
Simple instructions for how to use their unique link and where to put it.

## The Creation Prompt

```
I need to build an affiliate propaganda pack for [product]. 

My product: [describe]
Target customer: [describe]
Best customer results: [list 3-5 specific outcomes with numbers]
Affiliate profile: [describe who the affiliates are and what platforms they use]

Create:
1. A one-pager (written in affiliate's voice, not corporate)
2. 5 LinkedIn post templates they can customize
3. 3 email templates for affiliate newsletters
4. One customer success story formatted for social sharing
5. Instructions for using their tracking link

Everything should sound like a real person recommending something they genuinely use, not a press release.
```

## What Done Looks Like

- Every material is formatted and ready to share
- At least 5 social post templates exist
- At least 2 email templates exist
- Affiliates confirm the materials are easy to use and sound natural
- First affiliate posts using the materials within 7 days of receiving the pack
