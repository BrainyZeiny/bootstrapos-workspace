# Marketing Module 5 — Post Free Content

## What This Module Covers

This module is honest about its limits.

Content is a long game. It compounds slowly and then all at once. The founders who crack content marketing build audiences that generate customers for years without additional spend. When it works, it is one of the most powerful assets a company can own.

But cracking content requires genuine mastery and consistent execution. If you do not have both, content will drain your time without producing revenue.

## The Honest Take

If you want to learn content marketing, find a creator who has built an audience from zero in your niche and learn from them directly. Learn from someone who has done it, not from someone who read about it.

Content is not the right first move for most bootstrapped founders who need revenue now. It is a medium-to-long term play that rewards patience and consistency — neither of which are available to a founder who needs customers this month.

## When Content Makes Sense

- You have another channel generating revenue (so you are not depending on content for survival)
- You have a genuine point of view and the discipline to post consistently for 12+ months before expecting results
- Your product benefits from brand authority and organic discovery
- You can be genuinely interesting to the audience you are trying to reach

## What to Do Instead

If you are early and need revenue now:

1. Cold outreach — see /knowledge/marketing/module-2-cold-outreach.md
2. Paid ads — see /knowledge/marketing/module-3-paid-ads.md
3. Affiliate marketing — see /knowledge/marketing/module-4-affiliate-marketing.md

## If You Commit to Content

Find the best creator in your space who has built from zero. Study their format, their cadence, their angle. Do not copy them. Understand what is working and why, then apply that understanding to your own original perspective.

Post consistently for at least six months before evaluating whether it is working. Most people quit in month two.
