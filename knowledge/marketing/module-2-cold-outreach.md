# Marketing Module 2 — Cold Outreach

## What This Module Covers

Cold outreach in 2026 is brutal. Response rates are close to zero. Spam filters are ruthless. The founders who win are the ones who pair cold email with cold calling using site visit signals.

The secret: send the email, track who visits the site after reading it, call them the moment they come back to look. They already know who you are. The call does not feel cold anymore. Email is the warmup. The site visit is the signal. The call is the close.

Hivemind runs 15 domains, 45 inboxes, sends thousands of emails per day. Response rate around 0.5%. The system helped take Hivemind to $3.4M ARR in under six months — not because cold email alone works, but because the system knows exactly when to pick up the phone.

Lessons cover:

1. **Is cold outreach right for your business?** — High-ticket, niche, B2B only. If you are not selling something over $3K ACV to a definable list of companies, pick a different path.

2. **The only software worth using** — What actually works for sequencing, inbox management, and tracking. What to avoid.

3. **Domain and inbox setup** — How to avoid spam filters. Warming up domains. The infrastructure that makes volume possible without landing in spam.

4. **Copy that still gets replies in 2026** — The framework. Short. Specific. One pain. One claim. One ask.

5. **AI personalization at scale** — How to personalize every email to the specific person without sounding like a robot and without doing it manually.

6. **The email + call pairing system** — Site visitor tracking. How to know when someone opens an email and visits your site. How to route that signal to a caller in real time.

7. **Building it so it runs without you** — The system architecture. What gets automated. What requires a human. How to manage it ongoing.

## Infrastructure Requirements

- 15+ domains (one primary brand domain, rest are variations)
- 3 inboxes per domain
- Proper DNS: SPF, DKIM, DMARC on every domain
- Domain age: ideally 3+ months before sending
- Warming period: 2-4 weeks before full volume
- Site visitor tracking pixel on your landing page

## The Pairing System

1. Lead visits your site after reading a cold email
2. Site visitor tracking identifies the company (IP + reverse lookup)
3. Signal routes to sales rep within minutes
4. Rep calls with context: "I saw you were checking us out..."
5. The call converts at dramatically higher rates than cold calls alone
