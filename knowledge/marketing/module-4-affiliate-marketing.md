# Marketing Module 4 — Affiliate Marketing

## What This Module Covers

Hivemind has never spent a dollar on ads. Every customer came through affiliate marketing. $3.4M ARR in under six months. 120 affiliates. $333 CAC. $150K ACV. 450:1 CLV:CAC ratio.

Affiliate marketing is the most underrated growth channel in B2B. When it works, nothing touches it.

But it only works if you do two things right:

1. Build a system that finds the right affiliates and keeps them posting without chasing them.
2. Sell affiliates on your vision, not your commission structure. The affiliates who drive real revenue are motivated because they genuinely believe in what you are building.

Lessons cover:

1. **Is affiliate right for your business?** — High ACV, B2B, identifiable affiliate audience. If your average contract value is under $5K this channel may not pencil out. If it is over $10K, this might be your best channel.

2. **Finding the right affiliates** — Not influencers with big audiences. People who have the right audience. The difference is crucial. A recruiter with 2,000 followers who are all HR directors is more valuable than an influencer with 200,000 followers who are not buyers.

3. **The vision sell** — How to pitch affiliates on your mission, not your commission. What to say on the call. What to send afterward. How to make them feel like co-founders, not contractors.

4. **The infrastructure** — Tracking links. Commission structure. Payout timing. The tools that make it manageable at scale.

5. **Keeping affiliates active** — The system for staying in front of affiliates without annoying them. Monthly updates. Wins they can share. Community elements that keep them engaged.

6. **Scaling** — How to go from 10 affiliates to 120. What changes. What stays the same.

## The Commission Structure

For high-ACV B2B:
- 10-20% of first year contract value
- Pay on close, not on lead
- Cap at a reasonable number so margins hold

## The Affiliate Profile

Ideal affiliate for Hivemind:
- Runs a recruitment agency or is a solo recruiter
- Has a LinkedIn following of HR directors, talent leaders, or founders
- Posts regularly and has real engagement
- Has already placed candidates at companies like your buyers
