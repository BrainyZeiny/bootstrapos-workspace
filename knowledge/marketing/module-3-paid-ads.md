# Marketing Module 3 — Paid Ads

## What This Module Covers

RocketDevs hit $9.3M ARR and paid ads were the engine. $90K/month on Reddit at 18x ROAS. $35K/month on Meta at 16x lifetime ROAS. Google Search targeting niches that competitors were ignoring.

Most founders run ads wrong. They boost a post with no tracking. They run the same creative for six months. They spend $10K and get nothing back and decide ads do not work.

Ads work. Running them wrong does not.

Lessons cover:

1. **Where to spend your first dollar** — Which platform to start on, how much to budget, what to expect from the first 30 days.

2. **Technical fundamentals** — Pixel installation, conversion tracking, UTM parameters. You cannot optimize what you cannot measure. These are non-negotiable.

3. **The 16 persuasion fundamentals** — The frameworks behind a 16x lifetime ROAS. How to write copy that converts. What makes someone click.

4. **Reddit ads** — Everything learned from spending $90K/month. Targeting. Creative formats. What works in Reddit's unique environment. The exact strategy behind 18x ROAS.

5. **Google Search** — How to target niches where competitors are not bidding. The strategy that generates traffic at dramatically lower CPC than obvious keywords.

6. **Meta ads** — Everything learned from spending $35K/month at 16x lifetime ROAS. Campaign structure. Creative testing. Scaling winners. Killing losers fast.

7. **Landing pages that convert** — The structure that turns ad traffic into paying customers. The elements that cannot be missing.

## The Platform Decision

**Reddit:** Best for niches with strong subreddit communities. Tech, startup, finance, gaming. $50-100 minimum daily budget to learn.

**Meta:** Best for consumer or broad B2B. Visual products. Strong demographic targeting. Start with $50/day and test before scaling.

**Google Search:** Best for high-intent searches. People looking for what you sell. More expensive per click but higher buyer intent.

**Where to start:** Start with one platform. Master it. Then expand.

## Creative Strategy

Test everything. Run 3-5 creative variations at once. Kill losers in 7 days. Scale winners without mercy. Never fall in love with your creative. The data decides.
