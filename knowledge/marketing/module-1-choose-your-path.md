# Marketing Module 1 — Choose Your Path

## What This Module Covers

Most founders fail at marketing not because they are bad at it but because they try to do everything at once. They spread themselves across four channels and get zero traction on any of them.

The Hormozi Core Four: there are only four ways to get customers.

1. Reach out to them cold (cold outreach)
2. Post free content (organic content)
3. Run paid ads
4. Build organic search (SEO)

Pick one. Go deep. Master it before you touch another.

This module helps you make that choice and commit to it.

Lessons cover:

1. **The four paths** — What each one is, how long it takes, how much it costs, what kind of business it works best for.

2. **Cold outreach** — Best for: high-ticket, niche, B2B. You know exactly who your customer is. You can reach them directly. The system that took Hivemind to $3.4M ARR in under six months.

3. **Paid ads** — Best for: consumer or broad B2B, you have a product that converts on a landing page, you have at least $3-5K/month to spend. The system that took RocketDevs to $9.3M ARR.

4. **Affiliate marketing** — Best for: B2B with high ACV, you can find people with existing audiences of your buyers. 120 affiliates, $333 CAC, $150K ACV, 450:1 CLV:CAC.

5. **Content** — Long game. Compounds slowly then all at once. Requires genuine consistency. Not the right first move for most bootstrapped founders who need revenue now.

## The Decision Framework

**Selling to businesses (B2B) with high ticket price ($5K+ ACV)?**
Start with cold outreach or affiliate marketing.

**Selling to businesses with lower ticket price or broader market?**
Start with paid ads.

**Selling to consumers?**
Start with paid ads or content.

**Building a marketplace?**
Cold outreach to supply side first. Then paid ads for demand.

## Proof Points

- RocketDevs: $9.3M ARR — paid ads (Reddit $90K/month 18x ROAS, Meta $35K/month 16x ROAS)
- Hivemind: $3.4M ARR in under six months — affiliate + cold outbound
- Hivemind affiliates: 120 active, $333 CAC, $150K ACV, 450:1 CLV:CAC ratio

## The Rule

Pick your path. Go deep. That is the whole game. Everything else is noise.
